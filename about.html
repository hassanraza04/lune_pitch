About Lune

Built to make transaction data finally useful.

We help banks, neo-banks, and fintechs turn the data they already have into experiences customers can feel, and revenue they can measure.

Our mission

Make every transaction count.

For most banks, the gap between what they know about a customer and what their customer can feel is enormous. Transactions are categorized poorly or not at all; merchant data is messy; personalization stops at the first name. Lune closes that gap.

We sit between your transaction stream and your customer experience layer, turning noise into signal in real time, at scale, and in any market.
