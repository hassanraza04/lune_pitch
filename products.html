The Lune Platform

One platform. Every transaction signal.

Three composable modules built on a single real-time data layer. Use them independently, or together, to deliver banking experiences your customers can feel.

Enrich

Clean, contextual transactions in real time.

Raw transaction strings get transformed into structured, categorized data, complete with merchant identity, logos, location, and carbon impact. Built for global enrichment with local merchant graphs.

  • 99% categorization coverage
  • Merchant logos, contact info, and verified locations
  • Carbon footprint per transaction
  • Recurring detection and subscription matching

Engage

White-label financial UI your customers love.

Embeddable spending insights, goal-setting, budgets, and carbon impact visualizations. Built mobile-first and ready to drop into your app. Customers come back for the experience; you keep the data.

  • Drop-in widgets and full white-label components
  • Goal tracking, smart budgets, and proactive nudges
  • Carbon and sustainability tracking
  • Full RTL support, Arabic-first design

Target

Precision marketing built on real behavior.

Move beyond static personas. Target uses live transaction patterns to segment customers, predict intent, and trigger contextual offers. Campaigns convert because they're grounded in what people actually do.

  • Behavior-based audience segmentation
  • Predictive churn, upsell, and life-event detection
  • Card-linked offers with lift attribution
  • No-code campaign studio for marketing teams
