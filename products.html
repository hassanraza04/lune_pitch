The Lune Platform

One platform. Every transaction signal.

Three composable modules built on a single real-time data layer. Use them independently, or together, to deliver banking experiences your customers can feel.

Enrich

Localized enrichment, global coverage.

Lune transforms raw transaction strings into structured, contextual records in real time. Standardized brand logos, localized merchant names, precise categorization, geolocation, and carbon impact, delivered through a single API.

  • 20+ consumer categories, 121+ subcategories
  • Brand logos and merchant names in multiple languages, updated daily
  • Carbon footprint estimated per transaction
  • Recurring payments and subscriptions automatically identified

Target

Smarter recommendations, stronger conversions.

Influence customer behavior with AI-powered, data-backed nudges. Target analyzes real spending patterns and recommends the right product at the right moment, so the offer that lands is the one your customer was already on their way to.

  • Rule-based: precise conditions across categories, transaction types, and segments
